INDUCT VR  is an upcoming Virtual Reality training platform aimed at Universities and Vocational Schools. Learn carpentry, welding, 3D printing, and other specialized fabrication tools in the safety and comfort of your virtual space. Inspired by Instructables and Autodesk's Pier 9 , InductVR aims to introduce more people to tools and skills they might not have access to in the real world.

HOW IT WORKS

Create your account

Start by creating a user account and download the VR app from Steam or our website.

Get on Steam

Select your machines and inductions

Browse our growing cloud library of fully interactive workshop machinery. Each machine comes with a VR induction for basic functionality and HSE.

  •  Wood Shop : Mitre/Band Saw, Drill Presses, Routers, Hand tools
  •  Metal Shop : MIG Welding, Metal Saws, Plasma Cutter, Grinders
  •  Digital Manufacture : CNC machines, 3D printers, Robotic Arms

 

Start learning

Learn and teach

A step by step tutorial will guide you through each machine and its functionality, pinpoint specific hazards, and give you the skills and confidence to start practicing in the real world.
