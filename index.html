HOW IT WORKS

 

 

 

 

Choose a workshop layout

Choose one of the pre-made workshop layouts or import your own

Start by choosing a workshop layout that suits you and your project. You can pick between Traditional Workshop and Digital Manufacture, or import your own 3D model layout.

Learn More Start

Select your machines

Browse and import from our growing cloud library of fully intractable workshop machinery. Each machine comes with a VR induction for basic funtionality and HSE.

  •  Wood Shop : Mitre/Table/Band saw, Drill Presses and Hand tools
  •  Metal Shop : MIG Welding, Metal Saws, Plasma Cutter, Grinders
  •  Digital Manufacture : CNC machines, 3D printers, Robotic Arms

 

Start your inductions or record a lesson

Lear or teach

Start learning how to use the machines or record a workshop lesson for others. Using a simple user interface, you can record a demonstration of how to use a machine, pinpoint specific hazards, or create a step by step tutorial to share with others.

Learn More Start

Invite specialists, students or friends to join

Invite anyone to join you into your workshop.

Need a specialist for a one to one tutorial?
InductVR allows you to invite anyone from anywhere in the world to join your workshop.
